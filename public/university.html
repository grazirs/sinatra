Final Paper - TCC

Pressure drop study of a degasser in H2 production

This work aimed to study the ventilation system of a degasser in the production of hydrogen at high pressure, defining a pressure drop profile of the system.

Specific objectives: Carry out the pressure drop modeling of the degasser and the anti-mist/anti-dust filters, using the pressure drop moment correlations EI. Idel'cik. Identify the elements of greatest contribution that generate the pressure drop in the system. Find a fan model that meets the technical requirements in terms of static pressure and explosive atmosphere zone (ATEX).
